          🔷 The Internship Opportunity Tracker is a simple yet effective application designed to help students and job seekers manage and organize internship opportunities efficiently. The system allows users to perform basic CRUD operations—Create (Insert), Read (Display), Update, and Delete—on internship records. Each record typically includes details such as company name, role, location, eligibility criteria, application deadline, and status.

          🔷The main objective of this project is to provide a centralized platform where internship opportunities can be easily stored, updated, and accessed without confusion or data loss. By enabling quick insertion of new opportunities and flexible modification or removal of existing entries, the system ensures that users always have up-to-date information.

          🔷This tracker improves organization, reduces manual effort, and helps users efficiently manage multiple internship applications. It can be implemented using simple database management systems or programming languages such as Python, Java, or web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with backend support.
